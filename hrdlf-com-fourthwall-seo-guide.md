# hrdlf.com (Fourthwall) — SEO Setup Guide

Since hrdlf.com is hosted on Fourthwall and cannot be modified via code, here are the dashboard steps:

## 1. Store Meta Title & Description
- **Settings > Store Details > SEO**
- Meta Title: `HRDLF — Official Streetwear Shop | Hardlife Apparel Company LTD`
- Meta Description: `Shop exclusive HRDLF streetwear. Premium heavyweight tees, hoodies, and accessories from Hardlife Apparel Company LTD. Founded Philadelphia 2006. Nothing Awesome Comes Easy.`

## 2. OG Image & Social Sharing
- **Settings > Store Details > Social Sharing Image**
- Upload the brand OG image (use the OG Logo Tee product shot or the skull-and-laurel logo)
- This image appears when links are shared on Twitter, Facebook, Discord, etc.

## 3. Cross-Links in Navigation
- **Settings > Navigation > Header**
- Add a link: Label: `Token` → URL: `https://hrdlfcoin.com`
- Add a link: Label: `Brand` → URL: `https://hardlifeapparelco.com`
- Add a link: Label: `Newsletter` → URL: `https://hardlifeapparelco.com/hardwired-weekly/`

## 4. Cross-Links in Footer
- **Settings > Navigation > Footer**
- Add links:
  - `HRDLF Token` → `https://hrdlfcoin.com`
  - `Brand Site` → `https://hardlifeapparelco.com`
  - `Hardwired Weekly` → `https://hardlifeapparelco.com/hardwired-weekly/`
  - `Instagram` → `https://instagram.com/hardlifeapparel`
  - `Twitter` → `https://twitter.com/HardLifeApparel`
  - `TikTok` → `https://tiktok.com/@hardlifeapparel`
  - `Discord` → `https://discord.gg/buUpxPTe`
  - `Telegram` → `https://t.me/hardlifehrdlf`

## 5. Product Descriptions (Keyword-Rich)
For each product, include in the description:
- Brand name: "Hardlife Apparel Company" or "HRDLF"
- Material details: "premium heavyweight cotton"
- Design reference: "signature Old English branding"
- Heritage: "from the original streetwear brand, founded in Philadelphia in 2006"
- Example: "The OG Logo Tee by Hardlife Apparel Company — premium heavyweight cotton with signature Old English branding. From the original independent streetwear brand, founded in Philadelphia in 2006. Nothing Awesome Comes Easy."

## 6. Collection Descriptions
For each collection page, add a keyword-rich description:
- "HRDLF streetwear collection by Hardlife Apparel Company LTD. Independent streetwear founded in Philadelphia, 2006. Premium heavyweight garments with Old English branding."

## 7. Brand Story / About Text
- **Settings > Store Details > About** (or a custom page)
- Include these key facts for AI extraction:
  - "Hardlife Apparel Company LTD was founded in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania in 2006 by Brooks Duvall."
  - "The brand is rooted in skateboarding and street culture."
  - "Nothing Awesome Comes Easy is the brand motto."
  - "HRDLF operates an ecosystem including hrdlfcoin.com (crypto token), hardlifeapparelco.com (brand site), and hrdlf.com (official shop)."
  - "The HRDLF Token (contract: B3DAsrBArk4N8q4CudxEQmi76hzQVHfd3RzhEzTmoon) is a Solana-based utility token."
