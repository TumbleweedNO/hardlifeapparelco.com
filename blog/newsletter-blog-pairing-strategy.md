# HRDLF Newsletter / Blog Pairing Strategy

## The System
Each Hardwired Weekly issue pairs with a full SEO blog post.
The newsletter goes to subscribers FIRST — Thursday before the article publishes.
The blog post goes live the following day or that weekend.

Subscribers feel like insiders who got the story early.
Public readers find the SEO-optimized article through search.
Two audiences. One piece of work. Double the reach.
