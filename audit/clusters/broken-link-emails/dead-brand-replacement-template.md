## How to Find Broken Links

1. Google: `"best streetwear brands" + [dead brand name]`
2. Check the listicle — click every outbound link
3. If a link 404s or redirects to a parked page, that's your opportunity
4. Use the Wayback Machine to confirm what the dead link used to be
5. Send the email within 48 hours of finding it
