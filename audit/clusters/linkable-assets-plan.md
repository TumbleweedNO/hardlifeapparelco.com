### Asset 2: Interactive Tool — "Is Your Streetwear Brand Actually Independent?"

**Title:** The Streetwear Independence Checker

**Angle:** A simple quiz/calculator that scores a brand on independence criteria. 5 questions, instant result.

**Format:** Single WordPress page with embedded JavaScript quiz

**Questions:**
1. Who owns the brand? (Founder / Investor-backed / Corporate subsidiary)
2. Has the brand taken outside investment? (No / Seed/Angel / Series A+ / Acquired)
3. Who makes design decisions? (Founder / Design team reports to board / Licensed designs)
4. Does the brand restock sold-out products? (Never / Sometimes / Always)
5. Is the brand founder still involved in daily operations? (Yes / Partially / No)

**Scoring:**
- 5/5: "Verified Independent" — badge they can embed on their site
- 3-4/5: "Semi-Independent" — with explanation of what changed
- 0-2/5: "Corporate Streetwear" — not a bad thing, just different

**Length:** Single page, mostly interactive

**Who would link to it:**
- **Streetwear blogs and forums** — controversy bait (brands arguing their score)
- **Reddit r/streetwear** — community engagement, shareability
- **Marketing/branding blogs** — "clever brand positioning" case study
- **Other indie brands** — they'll link to prove their independence score

**How to pitch:**
Don't pitch directly. Seed it on Reddit, X, and streetwear Discord. Let it spread organically. When it does, pitch the story: "A 19-year-old streetwear brand built a tool to fact-check the industry."

**Effort:** MEDIUM (1-2 weeks for development + design)
**Link potential:** 10-30 referring domains, ongoing as people discover and share it
