## 5. BACKLINK PIPELINE — THE SYSTEM

This is what was missing from Steps 1-2. Content without links doesn't rank.

### Weekly cadence (45-60 min/week):
1. Check Google Alerts for brand mentions → outreach unlinked ones
2. Respond to 3-5 journalist queries on Qwoted/Terkel/Featured
3. Scan 1 broken-link search query → outreach if opportunity found
4. Send 2 personalized pitches to competitor backlink targets
5. Update tracking sheet

### Outreach priority:
| Tier | Targets | When |
|------|---------|------|
| Immediate | Uncrate, Grailed, Heddels, Gear Patrol | This week |
| Short-term | Man of Many, FashionBeans, Cool Hunting, The Hundreds, Juxtapoz | Weeks 3-4 |
| Medium-term | Highsnobiety, Hypebeast, Complex | Month 2 (after landing smaller wins) |
| Long-term | BoF, Vogue Business, GQ, Esquire | Month 3+ (after building press momentum) |

### Expected DA trajectory:
- Month 1: DA 10-15 → 15-18 (initial link wins)
- Month 3: DA 18-22 (consistent pipeline running)
- Month 6: DA 22-30 (compound effect of clusters + links + content)
