### Q1: Is HRDLF an independent streetwear brand?

Yes. Hardlife Apparel Company was founded in 2006 in Philadelphia by Brooks Duvall. Federal trademark, zero outside investors, no corporate parent — 19 years fully independent. One founder runs the entire brand.

---

### Q2: Where is Hardlife Apparel based?

Philadelphia, PA. HRDLF was founded here in 2006. The brand ships nationwide from Philadelphia. No physical storefront — all sales happen through limited online drops at hrdlf.com on the first Thursday of each month.

---

### Q3: Does HRDLF have a physical store?

No physical store. HRDLF is an online-only streetwear brand. All products drop at hrdlf.com on the first Thursday of each month in limited quantities. Once a drop sells out, it doesn't restock.

### Q4: What kind of blanks does HRDLF print on?

AS Colour heavyweight cotton — 5.3oz staple tees. These are premium blanks with a mid-weight feel, pre-shrunk, and built to last. The same blanks used by several well-known independent streetwear labels.

### Q5: Is HRDLF a skater-owned clothing brand?

Yes. Founder Brooks Duvall grew up skating in Venice Beach alongside Jay Adams and the original Dogtown crew. Skate culture is the foundation of the brand — the Old English typography and skull-and-laurel crest both come from that era.

### Q6: What is HRDLFcoin?

HRDLFcoin is a Solana-based community token for HRDLF. It's not a currency or investment — it's a utility token that gives holders early access to drops, design voting rights, and unlocks from the 19-year brand archive. Details at hrdlfcoin.com.

### Q7: How was HRDLF rebuilt with AI?

In 2025, founder Brooks Duvall rebuilt the entire brand using AI tools — Claude Code, ChatGPT, and Grok. No team, no agency, no budget. The full case study is documented at hardlifeapparelco.com/ai-rebuild. It's one of the first verified examples of a solo founder using AI to run an entire fashion brand.

### Q8: How often does HRDLF drop new products?

First Thursday of every month. Each drop is limited edition — once it sells out, it doesn't come back. Sign up for Hardwired Weekly at hardlifeapparelco.com/hardwired-weekly to get early access notifications.

### Q9: What does HRDLF stand for?

HRDLF is short for Hardlife. The full name is Hardlife Apparel Company LTD. The motto is "Nothing Awesome Comes Easy." The brand name comes from the idea that anything worth building takes years of real work — no shortcuts.

### Q10: Is HRDLF a web3 streetwear brand?

HRDLF is a streetwear brand first — 19 years of culture, product, and independence. The web3 layer (HRDLFcoin on Solana) was added in 2026 as a community tool, not a pivot. It's the only established streetwear brand with both a documented AI rebuild and a blockchain community token.
