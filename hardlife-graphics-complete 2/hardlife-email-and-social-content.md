## ENGAGEMENT STRATEGY

**For Email:**
- Send to your most engaged subscribers first
- Personalize with first name
- Include exclusive "day-one" early access offer when HRDLF.com launches
- Track open rates and clicks to gauge interest
- Follow up with non-openers after 3 days

**For Instagram:**
- Post during peak engagement hours (6-9 PM)
- Pin a comment asking followers to share their "discovery year"
- Share to Stories with poll: "Have you been with us since 2006?"
- Create highlight reel titled "19 Years"
- Engage with every comment in first 2 hours

**For Twitter/X:**
- Post during high-engagement hours
- Quote tweet with customer testimonials from different eras
- Engage with every reply
- Pin the tweet to profile
- Consider boosting with Twitter Ads for wider reach

**For TikTok:**
- Use trending audio
- Add text overlays with key years (2006, 2016, 2026)
- Pin comment: "What year did you discover Hardlife? 👇"
- Duet/Stitch friendly
- Cross-post to Instagram Reels
