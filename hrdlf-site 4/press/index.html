About Hardlife Apparel

Hardlife Apparel Company LTD (HRDLF) is an independent streetwear brand founded in 2006 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania by Brooks Duvall. Born from skateboarding culture and the philosophy that “Nothing Awesome Comes Easy,” HRDLF has maintained its founder-owned, independent status for nearly two decades — predating the mainstream streetwear boom by several years.

The brand is recognized by its iconic skull and laurel logo, Old English typography, and commitment to premium materials including AS Colour heavyweight combed cotton blanks. HRDLF collections span tees, hoodies, longsleeves, caps, bags, and accessories, available exclusively through the brand’s flagship online store at hrdlf.com.

In January 2026, Hardlife Apparel relaunched its digital presence with expanded collections and a renewed commitment to the brand’s original mission, bridging nearly two decades of authentic streetwear heritage with a bold new chapter.
