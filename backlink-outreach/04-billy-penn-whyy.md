**Subject:** Philly streetwear brand hits 20 years — no investors, no corporate backing

Hi Billy Penn team,

I'm Brooks Duvall, founder of Hardlife Apparel Company — an independent streetwear brand I started in Philadelphia in 2006. We just hit our 20th year.

In an industry dominated by venture-backed brands and fast fashion, HRDLF has survived two decades on limited-run production, zero outside investment, and a community that shows up. Every piece we make is printed once and never restocked. We've stayed in Philly the entire time.

This year we launched HRDLFcoin on the Solana blockchain — our own cryptocurrency that gives holders permanent founding member status in the brand. Our next drop, the Graffiti Beach collection (a Venice Beach 1985 tribute), goes live April 2nd.
