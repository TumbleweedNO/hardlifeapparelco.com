Our Story

Before the Hype.
Before the Industry.

Hardlife Apparel Company LTD was founded in 2006 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania — years before streetwear became a global phenomenon.

2006

The Beginning

In 2006, the streetwear landscape looked nothing like it does today. There were no billion-dollar valuations, no celebrity co-signs driving overnight sellouts, no algorithms turning brands into trends and trends into dust. There were just people who lived the culture — skaters, artists, musicians — and the brands that grew organically from that world.

Hardlife Apparel Company was born in that world. Founded by Brooks Duvall in Philadelphia, HRDLF started with a screen printer, a stack of premium blanks, and a philosophy that would become the brand’s DNA: Nothing Awesome Comes Easy.

The name “Hardlife” wasn’t aspirational — it was observational. It came from the skateboarding community, from the understanding that every trick you land is built on a hundred falls. Every great thing you build requires grinding through the parts that aren’t great. The brand was a reflection of that ethos, printed on the same heavyweight cotton tees that the crew wore to the park every day.

“The name came from skateboarding. Every trick you land is built on a hundred falls.”

Timeline

19 Years of Grinding

2006

Founded in Philadelphia

Brooks Duvall establishes Hardlife Apparel Company. Registers hardlifeapparelco.com and hrdlf.com. First collections printed on premium blanks and sold within the Philadelphia skateboarding community.

2006–2010

Building the Foundation

The brand grows through word-of-mouth within the skateboarding and street culture communities. The skull and laurel logo becomes recognizable in the Philadelphia scene. Collections expand beyond tees into hoodies and accessories.

2010–2015

The Underground Years

While the streetwear industry explodes around it, Hardlife Apparel stays true to its roots. The brand maintains its independent, founder-owned status. Social media presence grows organically through authentic community engagement.

2015–2024

Evolution & Innovation

The HRDLF abbreviated brand identity emerges. Exploration into Web3 and cryptocurrency with HRDLFcoin on Solana. The brand evolves its aesthetic from pure skate culture toward a more refined, high-end streetwear direction while maintaining its authentic foundation.

2026

The Relaunch

HRDLF.com relaunches with a new flagship store, expanded collections, and a renewed commitment to the original mission. Federal trademark applications filed. The Nordic Camo Duffle debuts. Nearly two decades of heritage meets a bold new chapter.

What We Stand For

The HRDLF Code

Authenticity Over
Algorithm.

We were here before the hype. We didn’t start this brand because a market report said streetwear was trending. We started because we lived it. Every design, every drop, every decision comes from that same place — real culture, not manufactured relevance.

Heritage Is
Earned.

You can’t buy 19 years of brand history. You can’t manufacture a 2006 domain registration. You can’t fake the relationships built through nearly two decades of being part of a community. Heritage isn’t a label you slap on — it’s a timeline you live through.

Quality Without
Compromise.

We print on AS Colour’s premium 5.3oz heavyweight combed cotton blanks — not because it’s the cheapest option, but because it’s the best. Pre-shrunk, side-seamed, double-needle topstitched. We’d rather sell fewer pieces at a higher standard than flood the market with disposable fashion.

Independent
Forever.

No venture capital. No corporate parent company. No outside investors telling us what to make. Hardlife Apparel Company is founder-owned and operated by the same person who started it in 2006. That’s not a limitation — it’s the entire point.
